About Home Assistant

Home Assistant is an open-source platform that allows you to control all your smart devices in one place. From lights and locks to sensors and thermostats, Home Assistant makes it easy to automate and manage your home seamlessly. Whether you're looking for simple automation or advanced, custom setups, Home Assistant offers a wide range of possibilities to make your life easier and more secure.
